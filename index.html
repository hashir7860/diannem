Charles Kovess

As well as being AIHA President, Charles Chairs the AIHA Sub Committees on Fibre and Hurd and Marketing and Commercialisation. Charles was the secretary of AIHA for 5 years prior to his election as President. Charles graduated from the University of Melbourne with a Bachelor of Laws with Honours in 1973 and gained his Master of Laws from Monash University in 1980. Charles is CEO and international Marketing Director of TCI (Textile and Composite Industries) and has been heavily involved since 2012 in ensuring that TCI achieves its global vision and goals. Charles is on the Executive Board of the global Federation of International Hemp Organisations (FIHO), as the representative of AIHA, a founding member of FIHO.
