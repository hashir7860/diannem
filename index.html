I provides culturally responsive maternal health training, birthing classes, program design, and community engagement strategies.

Supporting people, organisations, and communities to connect with care and culture.

I’m a midwife, researcher, speaker and advocate passionate about empowering culturally diverse communities through inclusive care and connection.

With over 30 years of experience, I offers personalised midwifery care and emotional resilience coaching, providing expert support through pregnancy, childbirth, and life’s transitions to help women thrive with confidence and strength.

Services and Offerings

Birthing & Pregnancy Classes

Dianne Morris offers gentle, culturally aware sessions for expectant mothers and parents — blending clinical knowledge with emotional insight to help you feel informed, empowered, and supported every step of the way.

Consulting

For healthcare organisations seeking to improve cultural safety, community engagement, or inclusive program design. Dianne Morris offers strategic advice grounded in real-world community work and lived experience.

Speaking & Facilitation

Dianne is an engaging speaker and skilled facilitator. Her sessions invite reflection, shared learning, and genuine connection — whether at conferences, panels, or grassroots community gatherings.

Comunity Projects

Dianne Morris supports and collaborates on community arts-based initiatives that honour cultural wisdom, centre culturally diverse perspectives, and prioritise holistic care.
